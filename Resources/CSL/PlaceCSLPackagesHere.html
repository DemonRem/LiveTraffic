CSL Packages

You must install CSL packages here, otherwise there will be no aircraft models to display and LiveTraffic will fail to start up.

Recommended are

  • the "Bluebell" OBJ8 CSL packages found at the X-Plane.org forums. Follow their instructions. Also download and install the BB_IVAO_vert_offsets_* files in the downloads as recommended for the X-IvAp users. LiveTraffic can handle them, too.
  • the Follow me car from X-CSL Package as a ground vehicle object.

Place the CSL packages into Resources/plugins/LiveTraffic/Resources/CSL.
